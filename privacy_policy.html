2. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

SocialHaven stores the following data only on your device (in the app's private storage):

A. User Preferences

  • Onboarding completion status

  • Purchase status (whether you've unlocked the full version)
  • Free trial usage count (maximum 3 free uses)

B. Fake Call Settings

  • Caller name and phone number (for display in fake call interface)
  • References to photos you've imported (stored in app's local directory)
  • References to audio files you've imported (ringtones, call audio)
  • Saved presets for quick access (up to 10 presets)

C. Media Files

  • Background images and avatars imported from your photo library
  • Custom ringtones and call audio files imported by you
  • All files are stored in the app's Documents directory on your device

Important: All this data remains on your device and is never uploaded to any server. If you delete the app, all data is permanently removed from your device.

3. Permissions and Why We Need Them

SocialHaven requests the following permissions:

A. Photo Library Access (Optional)

  • Purpose: Allows you to select photos from your library to use as fake call backgrounds or caller avatars
  • Usage: Only activated when you tap "Import Photo" buttons
  • Storage: Selected photos are copied to the app's local storage (not modified in your library)
  • Control: You can deny this permission; the app will work with default backgrounds

B. Siri & Shortcuts (Optional)

  • Purpose: Enables you to trigger fake calls using Siri voice commands or iOS Back Tap gestures
  • Usage: Only processes on-device shortcuts; no data sent to Apple or third parties
  • Control: You can choose not to set up shortcuts; the app works normally without them

C. Local Notifications (Optional)

  • Purpose: Reminds you when a scheduled fake call should arrive (if you scheduled a delayed call)
  • Usage: Notifications are created and delivered locally on your device
  • Control: You can deny notification permission in iOS Settings

No other permissions are requested or required.
