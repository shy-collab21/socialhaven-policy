Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

General Questions

Q: What is SocialHaven?

A: SocialHaven is an entertainment app that helps you simulate incoming phone calls. It's designed to help you gracefully exit awkward social situations or practice phone conversations. The app creates a realistic fake call interface that looks like an actual incoming call.

Q: Is SocialHaven free?

A: SocialHaven offers 3 free uses for new users. After that, you can unlock unlimited usage with a one-time purchase of $2.99 USD. No subscriptions, no recurring fees!

Q: Does SocialHaven make real phone calls?

A: No. SocialHaven is a simulation tool. It does NOT:

  • Make or receive actual calls
  • Connect to phone networks
  • Dial any numbers
  • Communicate with anyone

All "calls" are entirely simulated on your device for entertainment purposes.

Q: Is my data safe?

A: Absolutely! SocialHaven:

  • ✅ Does NOT collect any personal data
  • ✅ Does NOT upload anything to the internet
  • ✅ Stores all data locally on your device
  • ✅ Does NOT track your usage or behavior

See our Privacy Policy for full details.

App Features

Q: How do I trigger a fake call?

A: There are three ways:

1. Manual Trigger (Instant)

  • Open the app
  • Configure your fake call (name, number, background, etc.)
  • Tap "Trigger Fake Call Now"
  • The fake call will appear immediately

2. Scheduled Trigger (Delayed)

  • Open the app
  • Tap "Schedule Call"
  • Choose when you want the call to arrive
  • Lock your screen and wait
  • The call will appear at the scheduled time

3. Quick Shortcut (iOS Back Tap or Siri)

  • Set up a Siri Shortcut for SocialHaven
  • Enable "Back Tap" in iOS Settings > Accessibility
  • Triple-tap the back of your iPhone
  • Instant fake call appears!
Q: How do I set up the iOS Back Tap shortcut?

A:

  1. Open SocialHaven and complete the setup guide
  2. Open iOS Settings app
  3. Go to Accessibility > Touch > Back Tap
  4. Choose Triple Tap
  5. Scroll down and select SocialHaven (or "Trigger Fake Call")
  6. Done! Now triple-tap the back of your iPhone to trigger a call

Note: This feature requires iOS 14 or later and works on iPhone 8 and newer.

Q: What are Presets?

A: Presets are saved fake call configurations. You can save up to 10 presets with different:

  • Caller names (e.g., "Boss", "Mom", "Friend")
  • Phone numbers
  • Background images
  • Ringtones

This lets you quickly switch between different fake call scenarios!

Troubleshooting

Problem: App crashes when I open it

Solutions:

  1. Force quit and restart:
    • Swipe up from the bottom (or double-click Home button)
    • Swipe up on SocialHaven to close it
    • Reopen the app
  2. Restart your iPhone:
    • Press and hold the Power button
    • Slide to power off
    • Turn it back on
  3. Check for iOS updates:
    • Go to Settings > General > Software Update
    • Install any available updates
  4. Reinstall the app:
    • Delete SocialHaven
    • Reinstall from the App Store
    • Tap "Restore Purchase" to regain full access

If crashes persist, contact us at shy49045@gmail.com with your iOS version, iPhone model, and crash description.

Problem: Fake call doesn't appear

Solutions:

  1. Check Do Not Disturb mode: Make sure Do Not Disturb is OFF (Settings > Focus > Do Not Disturb)
  2. Check notification permissions: Go to Settings > SocialHaven > Enable Notifications
  3. For scheduled calls: Make sure you set a future time, keep the app in background, and lock your screen
  4. For Back Tap shortcuts: Verify Back Tap is enabled and try re-adding the shortcut

Problem: Can't import photos or audio

Solutions:

  1. Check permissions: Settings > SocialHaven > Photos > Select "All Photos"
  2. Supported formats: Photos (JPEG, PNG, HEIC), Audio (MP3, M4A, WAV)
  3. File size limits: Photos under 10MB, Audio under 5MB
  4. Try a different file: Some files may be corrupted or DRM-protected

Problem: My purchase didn't work

Solutions:

  1. Wait a few minutes: Apple's verification can take time
  2. Restore purchase: Tap "Restore Purchase" (requires internet)
  3. Check your Apple ID: Settings > [Your Name] > Media & Purchases
  4. Verify payment: App Store > Profile icon > Purchase History

If still not working, contact Apple Support first, then email us with your receipt.
